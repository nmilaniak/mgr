      AM2111

      • Share
      Add to compare list
      1 / 8

      The AM2111 Dino-Lite Basic is the entry level Dino-Lite USB microscope without compromise on product quality. It offers VGA resolution imaging (640x480), LED illumination, tough composite housing and a variable magnification of 10~70x and 200x.


      In addition, the series offers video frame rates up to 30 fps and the ability to save files in BMP, JPG and AVI format. Professional-quality imaging results can be viewed on any computer via high speed USB 2.0 output and the included DinoCapture (for PC) or DinoXcope (for MAC) software. The Dino-Lite AM2111 Basic is an excellent choice for hobbyists, schools, kids or any other cost sensitive application.
